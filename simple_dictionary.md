# Pronunciation Notes

NahVah pronunciation is intentionally phonetic and relatively shallow.

## General Rules

- Vowels are usually pronounced separately by syllable.
- NahVah avoids heavy English-style diphthongs.
- The letter `h` often softens or separates vowels rather than acting as a strongly pronounced consonant.
- Roots tend to preserve their pronunciation when compounded.
